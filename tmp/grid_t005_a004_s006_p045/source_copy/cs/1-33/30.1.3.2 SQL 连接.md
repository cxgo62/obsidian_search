## 概览
链接的类型
1. 内链接 
2. 外链接
3. 交叉链接

简单概括
1. 内链接就是取两个表中可以match上的行作为结果返回
2. 外链接就是以某个表为准，展示这个表的所有行，如果能匹配上另外一个表就把另外表的数据反范式化到结果中，如果匹配不上就给另外一个表的字段null值

## 样例数据
teacher表

id | teacher_id | teacher_name
---| ------ | ----
1 | teacher_id1 | teacher_name1
2 | teacher_id2 | teacher_name2

student表

id | student_id | teacher_id
---| --- | ---
1 | student_id1 | teacher_id1
2 | student_id2 | teacher_id3
## 内链接
对于两张表的字段做比较运算，可以认为是取两个表的交集
```sql
SELECT 	
	student.* teacher.* 
	FROM student INNER JOIN teacher
	on 	teacher.teacher_id = student.teacher_id 
```
内链接可以是隐式的，写成：
```sql
SELECT 	
	student.* teacher.* 
	FROM student s, teacher t
	WHERE t.teacher_id = s.teacher_id 
```
	
获得的结果是两张表中被指定字段的合并

id | student_id | teacher_id | teacher_name
---| --- | --- | ---
1 | student_id1 | teacher_id1 | teacher_name1


## 外链接
### 左链接
包含LEFT JOIN左边表的所有行，和内链接的区别是内连接只返回匹配的行，外链接中如果没有匹配的行就返回null字段
```SQL
SELECT 	
	student.* teacher.* 
	FROM student LEFT JOIN teacher
	on 	teacher.teacher_id = student.teacher_id 
```

返回的结果

id | student_id | teacher_id | teacher_name
---| --- | --- | ---
1 | student_id1 | teacher_id1 | teacher_name1
2 | student_id2 | teacher_id3 | null

### 右链接
左链接的反向操作，返回右边的表的所有行
```SQL
SELECT 	
	student.* teacher.* 
	FROM student RIGHT JOIN teacher
	on 	teacher.teacher_id = student.teacher_id 
```
返回的结果

id | student_id | teacher_id | teacher_name
---| --- | --- | ---
1 | student_id1 | teacher_id1 | teacher_name1
2 | null | teacher_id2 | teacher_name2

### 全链接（全外链接）
返回左右表的全部数据，相当于左链接和右链接的数据的合并

左链接的反向操作，返回右边的表的所有行
```SQL
SELECT 	
	student.* teacher.* 
	FROM student FULL JOIN teacher
	on 	teacher.teacher_id = student.teacher_id 
```
返回的结果

id | student_id | teacher_id | teacher_name
---| --- | --- | ---
1 | student_id1 | teacher_id1 | teacher_name1
2 | student_id2 | teacher_id3 | null
3 | null | teacher_id2 | teacher_name2

## 交叉链接
交叉链接是两个表的笛卡尔积，返回左表中的每一行和右边中所有行的组合，返回的行数 = 左表长度 * 右表长度
```sql
SELECT * student CROSS JOIN teacher
```
返回值

id | student_id | teacher_id | teacher_id | teacher_name
---| --- | --- | ---
1 | student_id1 | teacher_id1 | teacher_id1 | teacher_name1
2 | student_id1 | teacher_id1 | teacher_id2 | teacher_name2
3 | student_id2 | teacher_id3 | teacher_id1 | teacher_name1
4 | student_id2 | teacher_id3 | teacher_id2 | teacher_name2

返回的结果可以使用WHERE进行条件过滤
```sql
SELECT * FROM student s CROSS JOIN teacher t WHERE s.teacher_id = t.teacher_id
```
可以返回和inner join一样的结果，但还是会事先在内部生成上面的所有数据行
